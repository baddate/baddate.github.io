    🔖Tips 🔖Git

OS: archlinux terminal: alacritty

  1. Open terminal, and entering ssh-keygen

  2. You can find the SSH Key pair in ~/.ssh directory(note: id_rsa is private key and id_rsa.pub is public key.)

  3. Log in github, go to "Account Settings", and you can find "SSH Keys"page. Then click "New SSH Key", enter something for title and pasting contents of id_rsa.pub in the Keytextfield.
